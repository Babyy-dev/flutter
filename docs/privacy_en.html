This Privacy Policy explains how Steel Verdict ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

You may use the app anonymously or register with an email address. If you register, we store your email address and a unique user ID in Firebase Authentication.

Game Data

We store the following in Firebase Firestore:

  • Your race name and stats (Strength, Intellect, Skill, Magic, Art, Life)
  • Battle history, results, and AI-generated battle reports
  • Ticket balance and subscription status
  • PvP match data (strategies submitted, outcomes)
  • War chronicle entries you generate
  • Favorites you have saved

Battle Strategy Text

When you submit a battle, the strategy text you type is sent to a third-party AI service (Google Gemini or Anthropic Claude) to generate the battle report. This text is processed on our Cloud Functions servers and forwarded to the AI provider. We do not permanently store your raw strategy text beyond what is saved in your battle history.

Local Storage

Some preferences and cached data are stored locally on your device using Hive. This data never leaves your device unless synced to Firestore.

Advertising Data

The app uses Google AdMob to display rewarded ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant advertisements. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Purchase Data

If you make in-app purchases (subscriptions or ticket packs), transactions are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We receive a receipt token to validate the purchase but do not receive or store your payment card information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and deliver the game experience
  • To generate AI battle reports using your submitted strategy
  • To manage your ticket balance and subscription entitlements
  • To match you with opponents in PvP mode
  • To display rewarded advertisements and credit earned tickets
  • To validate in-app purchases

3. Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Functions, Remote Config: firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Gemini API — AI battle report generation: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI battle report generation (Epic mode): anthropic.com/privacy
  • Google AdMob — Rewarded advertisements: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play / Apple App Store — In-app purchases

4. Data Retention

Your account and game data are retained for as long as your account is active. Anonymous accounts and inactive accounts may be deleted after an extended period of inactivity. PvP matches are automatically deleted after 24 hours of inactivity (TTL policy).

5. Data Security

We use Firebase security rules to restrict access to your data. Only authenticated users can read or write their own records. Cloud Functions use server-side validation before processing any battle request.

6. Children's Privacy

Steel Verdict is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below. Upon request, we will delete your Firestore records and Firebase Auth account within 30 days.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
